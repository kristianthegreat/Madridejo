# 🌹 KristianTheGreat: Dodge the Creeps

## 🎮 Description

**KristianTheGreat: Dodge the Creeps** is a fast-paced survival dodge game developed using the **Godot Engine**.
Players must navigate a dangerous arena filled with flying nightmare creatures and survive for as long as possible.

You play as **KristianTheGreat**, a lone survivor trapped in a distorted dimension where reality bends and enemies take bizarre, chaotic forms.

Among the threats you will face:

* ⚡ A hostile energy entity capable of rapid movement and laser attacks
* 🌀 A dimensional phantom that shifts unpredictably across space
* 🔥 A corrupted floating core that emits dangerous bursts of energy

Each enemy behaves erratically, especially those affected by dimensional distortion, forcing players to react quickly, master movement, and stay focused under pressure.

This project highlights core game development concepts such as:

* Real-time movement
* Collision systems
* Enemy spawning logic
* Animation control
* Experimental multi-directional (4D-inspired) movement mechanics

It also serves as a foundation for future expansion and improvements.

---

## ✨ Features

* ⚡ **Dynamic Dodge Gameplay** – Survive against continuous waves of airborne enemies
* 👾 **Unique Enemy Designs** – Bizarre dimensional anomalies with unpredictable behavior
* 🎯 **Responsive Movement System** – Smooth 4-directional movement with animation blending
* ♾️ **Endless Survival Mode** – The longer you last, the higher your score
* 🏆 **Real-Time Score Tracking** – Challenge your best performance
* 🧠 **Simple but Challenging Mechanics** – Easy to learn, difficult to master

---

## 🛠️ Development Notes

This game was built as a learning project focusing on gameplay systems and debugging.

One major challenge encountered:

* Collision detection inconsistencies after restarting the game

To address this:

* Adjustments were made to physics handling and node resetting
* A custom **4-direction movement checker system** was implemented
  to improve animation transitions and ensure consistent control
